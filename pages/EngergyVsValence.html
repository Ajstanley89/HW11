Energy vs Valence

Country Music Ain't What It Used To Be...

There was a positive correlation beween the energy of a song and its valence (how happy or sad the song is). This relationship is intuitive. The better a song makes you feel, the more it energizes you!

However, there was one unexpected finding: Country music was signiificantly happier than the other songs. Country music used to be about sorrow and drinking away your problems; what happened? It seems the country stars of today are more about haviing a good time driving trucks and drinking beer!
