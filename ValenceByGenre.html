Valence by Genre

Does It Pay To Be Sad?

Do people write more happy songs or more sad songs these days? According to our data, it's both, depending on the genre. However, the more popular genres tended to be slightly biased toward the sad-side.

We observed a significant difference in the valence of all genres we studied. Country music tended to be happier, while rap and the Hot-100 songs were on the sadder side. Merle Haggard is probably scratching his head in his grave trying to figure out what happened to Country music. If he were still around, I'm sure we'd see the fightin' side of him.
